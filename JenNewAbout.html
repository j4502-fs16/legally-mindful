About Us

Our Mission Statement

The Mindfulness in Law Society (MILS) seeks to improve the mental health and well-being of lawyers, law students, faculty, Judges, and others in the legal community across the nation by building a community of like-minded professionals and promoting mindful practices.

What We Do

MILS exists to offer centralized resources for all those seeking to create or enhance a mindfulness practice or program for themselves, their law firms, or within the legal academy.
